privacy policy.

last updated: april 2026. riveting stuff.

who we are

we're thatswassupps. we make streetwear and a game called floppy crops. this policy covers the game (web and ios) and our website at thatswassupps.com.

what we collect

almost nothing. seriously.

when you play floppy crops and submit a score to the leaderboard, you can enter a display name. that's optional. if you skip it, we call you "anon." we store that display name and your score. that's it.

what we don't collect

no email addresses. no phone numbers. no location data. no device identifiers. no cookies. no tracking pixels. no analytics. no ads. no selling your data. none of that.

where the data lives

leaderboard data (display names and scores) is stored on supabase, a hosted database service. it's publicly visible on the leaderboard — that's the whole point.

third party services

the game sends high score notifications to our discord server via webhook. no personal data is included beyond the display name you chose.

children

the game is rated 4+ and we don't knowingly collect personal information from children. the optional display name is not considered personal information since it's a freely chosen alias.

your rights

want your scores removed from the leaderboard? email us at hello@thatswassupps.com and we'll handle it.

changes

if we ever change this policy, we'll update this page. but honestly, we're probably never going to collect more data. that sounds like work.
